About me

Hi! My name is Blake, and I am a current student at Epicodus. Here is a little information about me.

I have lived in Portland, OR for about 3 years, but I am originally from Greensboro, NC. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro in 2008 with a BS in Psychology. I have worked in Mental Health and Social Services for the last 5 years. Most recently, I worked for Multnomah County's Aging, Disabilities and Veteran's Services Division as a Case Manager.
